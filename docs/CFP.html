Call for Papers

Format

The Software Engineering Track is a three-day event in the program of ITNG 2018 at Las Vegas - USA. The planned agenda interleaves presentation of accepted full and short papers, as well as discussions to stimulate participants. We intend to solicit both technical (up to 6 pages) and short papers (up to 2 pages) following the Springer format style.

These papers will be peer-reviewed by the program committee. Participants are furthermore encouraged to read all accepted papers before the track. It is our intention to receive submissions from researchers new to the field as well as experienced researchers and practitioners.

We want to offer a platform for discussing their ideas and receiving feedback on them. This will be supported by question and answer sessions as well as by group discussions.

Topics of Interest

Examples of topics targeted by this Software Engineering track include, but are not limited to:

  1. • Agile software development

  2. • Cloud and service-oriented computing

  3. • Cooperative, distributed, and collaborative software engineering

  4. • End-user software engineering

  5. • Human factors and social aspects of software engineering

  6. • Mining software engineering repositories

  7. • Model-driven engineering

  8. • Program comprehension and visualization

  9. • Requirements engineering

  10. • Software architecture

  11. • Software evolution and maintenance

  12. • Software testing

  13. • Software quality

  14. • Tools and environments
