ITNG Software Engineering Track 2019

The software engineering track at ITNG 2019 is intended to be a forum for researchers and practitioners from the industry and the academia to present and discuss innovations and solutions to concrete software engineering challenges. It is an opportunity for exchanging new ideas, fostering long-term innovations, networking and forging collaborations for addressing software engineering research directions.
